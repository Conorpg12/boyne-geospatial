Meet the Founder

Conor Gleeson

Conor is the founder of Boyne Geospatial and a GIS professional with hands-on experience across the full spatial workflow - from field data collection and UAV survey through to spatial analysis, database management, and cartographic production.

Most recently, Conor spent two years working as a GIS Analyst for an environmental consultancy in rural New South Wales, Australia. In that role he led fieldwork programmes across remote and challenging terrain, developed spatial data collection methodologies, and delivered environmental analysis outputs to regulatory and project management standards. Working in the Australian context sharpened his ability to operate independently, adapt workflows to diverse environments, and deliver under real project pressure.

Alongside his mapping work, Conor has a genuine passion for aerial imagery and what can be seen from the sky. That interest led him to explore what consumer-grade drones could actually deliver in a professional context - and the answer surprised him. Combined with strong spatial fundamentals, a well-flown consumer drone can produce imagery and orthomosaics that add real value to a project at a fraction of the cost of traditional aerial survey. It's that practical thinking - squeezing insight from accessible tools without compromising on quality - that runs through everything Boyne Geospatial does.

Now back in Ireland and based in Navan, County Meath, he founded Boyne Geospatial to bring that international experience to clients across Ireland - combining technical depth with practical, field-tested rigour. He provides GIS mapping, spatial analysis, and drone imagery services across environmental, infrastructure, land development, and heritage sectors, committed to delivering work that is precise, clearly communicated, and built to last.

Our Story

Spatial Expertise, Grounded in Ireland

Boyne Geospatial was founded with a clear purpose: to bring the full power of modern GIS technology to the clients who need it most. Named after the River Boyne - one of Ireland's most historically mapped and studied landscapes - we carry that sense of place and precision into everything we do.

We operate nationally across Ireland and take on international projects where our expertise adds value. Our client base spans government agencies, local authorities, environmental consultancies, infrastructure developers, and land-use planners.

Whether you need a single map produced to a tight deadline or a complete spatial data strategy built from the ground up, we bring the same level of care, technical rigour, and cartographic craft to every project.
