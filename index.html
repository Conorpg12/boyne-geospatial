About Us

Rooted in the Boyne Valley

Boyne Geospatial is a visual storytelling studio based in Navan, County Meath - on the banks of the River Boyne and surrounded by some of Ireland's most storied landscape.

We combine drone aerial work with ground-level camera to tell the stories of the people, places, and projects that deserve to be seen. Every shoot is planned with the kind of location intelligence that only comes from truly knowing the terrain.
