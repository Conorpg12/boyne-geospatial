What We Offer

GIS Mapping, Spatial Analysis & Drone Survey Services Ireland

End-to-end spatial solutions - from field data collection through to published cartographic outputs - tailored to your project's requirements.

Core Service Areas

We work with government agencies, environmental consultancies, infrastructure developers, and land-use planners across Ireland and internationally.

GIS Mapping & Map Production

Boyne Geospatial produces precision cartographic outputs for government bodies, environmental consultancies, infrastructure firms, and land developers across Ireland. Every map we produce is customised to your project — its purpose, audience, and scale. Whether you need a single-page planning exhibit or a full suite of thematic outputs, we tailor the approach to suit. Get in touch to discuss your project.

Spatial Analysis

Our spatial analysis service turns raw geographic data into actionable insight. We work with clients on habitat mapping, land suitability analysis, species polygon mapping, catchment analysis, and site selection — including outputs for planning applications under the Planning & Development Act and National Planning Framework requirements. If your project requires quantitative spatial reasoning backed by robust GIS methodology, we can help.

  • Site suitability and land-use analysis (MCDA)
  • Planning constraint and opportunity mapping
  • Species polygon and habitat mapping
  • Buffering, clipping, intersection, and proximity analysis
  • Infrastructure and utilities corridor analysis
  • Asset mapping
  • Automation of repeatable spatial workflows

Drone Imagery & Content

We provide professional UAV aerial imaging services across Ireland — fully compliant with Irish Aviation Authority regulations. Professional aerial photography and video without the helicopter price tag. Whether you need structured progress documentation, social content, a framed print of somewhere special, or a survey of somewhere too difficult or dangerous to access on foot — a drone can get there. Get in touch to discuss what suits your project.

  • Construction and development progress packages at key project stages
  • Property photography from the air
  • Survey of hazardous or hard-to-access areas
  • Social media video and photo content
  • Framed aerial prints of any location, on request
  • Basic orthomosaics of small to medium sized areas
