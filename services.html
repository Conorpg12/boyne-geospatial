What We Offer

GIS Services

End-to-end spatial solutions — from field data collection through to published cartographic outputs — tailored to your project's requirements.

Core Service Areas

We work with government agencies, environmental consultancies, infrastructure developers, and land-use planners across Ireland and internationally.

Topographic & Thematic Mapping

Precision topographic and thematic maps produced to professional cartographic standards — suitable for planning submissions, environmental reports, and public-facing publications.

  • Large-scale site surveys and topographic plans
  • Land cover and land-use mapping
  • Geological and environmental thematic maps
  • Multi-scale outputs from 1:500 to 1:250,000
  • Print-ready PDF and digital web formats

Spatial Data Collection & Processing

Field data acquisition using UAV/drone survey, GPS, and remote sensing, combined with rigorous GIS data processing pipelines.

  • UAV aerial survey and photogrammetry
  • GPS field data collection and validation
  • LiDAR data processing and DEM generation
  • Satellite and aerial imagery analysis
  • Spatial database design and management

Custom Map Production

Bespoke cartographic design for any purpose — from high-resolution print atlases to interactive web map applications built for public or client use.

  • Print atlases and planning exhibition boards
  • Web maps and interactive GIS applications
  • Infographic and data-visualisation mapping
  • Branded cartographic templates
  • Multi-language and accessibility-ready outputs

Site Suitability & Land-Use Analysis

Multi-criteria spatial analysis to assess land suitability for development, conservation, or infrastructure — evidenced, repeatable, and defensible.

  • Multi-criteria suitability modelling (MCDA)
  • Planning constraint and opportunity mapping
  • Flood risk, slope, and environmental overlay analysis
  • Renewable energy and infrastructure site selection
  • Strategic Environmental Assessment (SEA) support

Infrastructure & Utilities Mapping

Asset mapping, network routing, and utility corridor analysis for roads, water, energy, and telecoms infrastructure projects.

  • Underground utility survey integration
  • Road and transport network mapping
  • Pipeline and cable corridor analysis
  • Asset management GIS database build
  • Clearance, buffer, and proximity analysis
