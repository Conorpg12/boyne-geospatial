What We Offer

GIS Services

End-to-end spatial solutions - from field data collection through to published cartographic outputs - tailored to your project's requirements.

Core Service Areas

We work with government agencies, environmental consultancies, infrastructure developers, and land-use planners across Ireland and internationally.

GIS Mapping & Map Production

Precision topographic and thematic mapping through to bespoke cartographic outputs - produced to professional standards for planning submissions, environmental reports, infrastructure projects, and public-facing publications.

  • Large-scale site surveys and topographic plans
  • Land cover, land-use, and thematic mapping
  • Multi-scale outputs from 1:500 to 1:250,000
  • Print atlases and planning exhibition boards
  • Infographic and data-visualisation mapping
  • Branded cartographic templates

Spatial Analysis

Multi-criteria spatial analysis, site suitability modelling, and infrastructure assessment - turning complex geographic datasets into clear, evidence-based outputs that support planning, environmental, and engineering decisions.

  • Site suitability and land-use analysis (MCDA)
  • Planning constraint and opportunity mapping
  • Flood risk, slope, and environmental overlay analysis
  • Infrastructure and utilities corridor analysis
  • Asset mapping and network routing
  • Spatial database design and management

Drone Imagery & Content

High-resolution aerial photography and video captured by UAV - whether for survey work, construction progress, events, or simply a place that matters to you. If a location is hard to reach or needs to be seen from above, get in touch. Outputs are delivered georeferenced where required.

  • Construction and development progress photography
  • Large-format aerial prints of special or significant locations
  • Event and occasion aerial videography
  • Survey of inaccessible or hazardous areas
  • Georeferenced orthomosaics for visual and planning use
  • Orthophoto outputs ready for GIS
