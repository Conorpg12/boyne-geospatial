Our Services

Athlete Content

Your story, from the ground and from the air.

Athletes train in extraordinary places and push themselves in ways that most people never see. A running trail through the Boyne Valley at dawn. A cross-country course soaked in October rain. A hill sprint that a phone camera simply can't follow. That's where we come in.

We follow athletes with our drone in the air and our ground camera on the ground - capturing the training, the terrain, and the character behind the performance. The result is content that shows what an athlete is actually made of, not just a highlight reel of race-day results.

Our spatial background means we plan shoots with genuine knowledge of the landscape - understanding how the terrain, light, and environment interact to produce the best possible imagery. That's what makes athlete content from us different to what you'd get from a standard video crew.

What's Included

  • Drone tracking and aerial coverage of training sessions
  • Ground-level camera work during training and competition
  • Short social media reels (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
  • Mini documentary pieces - athlete profile and story format
  • Documentary-style interview segments
  • Terrain-intelligent shot planning for landscape and environment context

Who It's For

Individual athletes, teams, and coaches across any discipline - runners, cyclists, GAA players, swimmers, climbers - who want to build a personal brand, attract sponsorship, or simply document the journey with content that does justice to what they put in.
