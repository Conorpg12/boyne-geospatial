Our Services

New Homes & Property

Content that sells lifestyle, not just bricks.

First impressions sell homes. A drone flythrough of a new development, golden-hour photography of the surrounding landscape, a short story reel for social - these are the things that make someone stop scrolling and start imagining themselves there.

We work with developers, estate agents, and marketing teams to create visual content that goes beyond floor plans and front elevations. Flying with our drone and shooting ground-level on our ground camera, we capture the whole story - the setting, the scale, the feel of the place.

Whether it's a single-phase launch or an ongoing development campaign, we tailor the content to what you actually need - and plan every shoot with a close eye on the light, the landscape, and the story you're trying to tell.

What's Included

  • Aerial photography of sites and completed homes from our drone
  • Drone flythroughs and cinematic reveal shots
  • Ground-level photography with our ground camera - interiors, exteriors, surroundings
  • Short story reels edited for social media (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn)
  • Development launch photography packages
  • Large-format print-ready aerial imagery

Who It's For

Property developers, housebuilders, estate agents, and marketing agencies working on new home launches, show house openings, or any residential or commercial development that deserves to be seen at its best.
