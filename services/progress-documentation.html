Our Services

Progress & Development Documentation

A record of your project, from first sod to final handover.

Construction and development projects transform landscapes over months and years - and that transformation is rarely captured properly. A drone visit at key project stages builds a visual record that's useful for stakeholders, valuable for marketing, and genuinely compelling as a before-and-after story.

Flying with our drone, we document progress from consistent angles and elevations at each visit, giving you a clear picture of how a site evolves over time. Combined with ground-level photography on our ground camera, you get a complete record - aerial context and on-the-ground detail together.

We work flexibly around site access and project schedules. Whether it's monthly visits over a construction programme or targeted shoots at key milestones, we'll build a documentation package that works for your project.

What's Included

  • Regular aerial drone photography at defined project stages
  • Ground-level site photography at each visit
  • Consistent framing and elevation for before-and-after comparison
  • Georeferenced aerial imagery for visual context (indicative, not survey-grade)
  • Short progress video compilations for stakeholder and marketing use
  • High-resolution image delivery for print and digital use

Who It's For

Construction firms, developers, landowners, and project managers across Ireland who want a professional visual record of their development - for stakeholder reporting, planning purposes, marketing assets, or simply to capture the story of what was built and how.
