Our Services

Stories & Place

Every place has a story. We help tell it.

Some stories don't fit neatly into a category. A community event with decades of history. A landscape shaped by centuries of human activity. A heritage site that most people drive past without knowing what they're looking at. These are the stories that matter most — and the hardest ones to tell well.

We're based in the Boyne Valley — one of Ireland's most historically rich and visually striking landscapes — and that's where a lot of this work begins. But it extends across Ireland: communities, people, environments, and places that deserve a documentary eye and a camera that knows how to find the right frame.

Using the DJI Mavic Mini 4 Pro for aerial perspective and the Canon 90D for ground-level detail, we approach this work as editorial storytellers — interested in the subject, honest about the place, and patient enough to get the shot right.

What's Included

  • Editorial documentary content about people, communities and landscapes
  • Heritage and cultural site photography and video
  • Aerial landscape photography of the Boyne Valley and Irish countryside
  • Long-form narrative documentary pieces for brands and organisations
  • Community storytelling for tourism, cultural, and local authority projects
  • Short-form place-based content for social media and digital platforms

Who It's For

Tourism bodies, heritage organisations, local authorities, brands with a strong sense of place, and anyone who wants to tell a story about Ireland — its landscape, its communities, and the people who shape it.
